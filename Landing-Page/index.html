Types of Fruits

Apples

Apples are one of the most popular and widely consumed fruits worldwide, and for good reason. Not only are they delicious and versatile, but they also offer an array of health benefits.

Guava

Guava is a tropical fruit native to Central America, but it's now cultivated in many tropical and subtropical regions around the world.

Banana

Bananas are one of the most popular and widely consumed fruits worldwide. They belong to the genus Musa and are native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Pine Apples

Pineapples are tropical fruits known for their sweet and tangy flavor. They belong to the Bromeliaceae family and are native to South America, particularly Brazil and Paraguay.

Grapes

Grapes are small, round fruits that grow in clusters on vines belonging to the Vitis genus. They are believed to have originated in the Mediterranean region and are now cultivated in many parts of the world.

Oranges

Oranges are citrus fruits belonging to the Rutaceae family and are believed to have originated in Southeast Asia.
